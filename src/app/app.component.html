New book out soon

We are excited to anounce that we have partnered with Wild Things Publishing to release our first book with the working title "Wild Swimming Marine Explorer: Best Snorkelling and Underwater Adventures in Britain"

The book will be packed with information on the best sites to snorkel across Britain, complimented with inspiring photography, clear site finding advice and vital tips to get you started.
